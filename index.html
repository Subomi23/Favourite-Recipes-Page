Indomie (Noodles)

My way to cook noodles to make the taste very good and nourishing

Ingredients

  • Indomie Tables
  • Grounded pepper
  • Chicken/Turkey

Step-by Step instructions

  1. Put the noodles and its ingredients in a pot
  2. Add the grounded pepper to make it spicy
  3. Add the turkey or chicken in the same pot
  4. Add a little amount of water and cook it
  5. Then it is done

Cooking rice

My way to cook and boil rice

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Salt

Step-by Step instructions

  1. Wash the rice
  2. Add the rice into a pot and add boiling water
  3. Then add a pinch of salt
  4. For about 15-20 minutes, check if the rice is soft. If it is, drain and sieve it and it is ready to go
